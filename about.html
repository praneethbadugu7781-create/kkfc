OUR STORY

From a simple idea to your favorite food destination

Welcome to Kartikeya Ice Creams & KKFC — your destination for premium ice creams and crispy fried chicken in Kanchikacherla.

Experience the perfect combination of hot & spicy chicken and cool, refreshing ice creams — all under one roof.
